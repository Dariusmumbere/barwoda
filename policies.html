Accountability & Transparency

BARWODA — Bayira Rural Women's Development Association is fully registered under Kasese District Local Government. Our policies align with international best practices in anti-fraud, anti-corruption, safeguarding, risk management, and governance. Every document here represents our promise to donors, partners, and the communities we serve: integrity above all.
