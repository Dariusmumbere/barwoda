Our Direction

Vision & Goals

A clear vision rooted in equality. Concrete goals built from community needs. The roadmap BARWODA follows to transform Kasese District — and beyond.

VISION
🌟 Our Vision
A knowledgeable, skilled, and empowered women's community in Kasese District

…that actively participates in decision-making and drives sustainable development. We envision a future where every woman in Kasese District has the knowledge, skills, and confidence to shape her own life — and contribute meaningfully to her community.

GOAL
🎯 Our Goal
Unite and engage rural women in initiatives that transform their lives

To improve the standards of living for rural women while fostering a community free from violence — especially domestic violence — for sustainable development. We believe transformation happens when communities are organized, resourced, and empowered to act.

Strategic Framework

Four pillars of our approach

01
📖
Education & Literacy
Knowledge is the foundation of all empowerment. We invest in adult literacy, vocational training, and technical education to open doors that were previously closed.
02
⚖️
Rights & Safety
Every woman deserves to live free from violence. We build networks of community advocates and provide paralegal support to protect rights and challenge harmful practices.
03
🌱
Economic Empowerment
Livelihoods create dignity. Through livestock projects, tailoring, typing, and sanitary pad production, we generate real and lasting economic independence.
04
🌍
Environmental Stewardship
A healthy environment sustains communities. Our tree planting, climate-resilient farming, and conservation work ensure a livable future for generations to come.
Core Objectives

What we are committed to

These are not abstract aspirations — they are the measurable commitments that guide every program, every partnership, and every decision BARWODA makes.

1
Eliminate Poverty, Unemployment, Hunger, Disease & Illiteracy

Promote women-led initiatives that tackle the full spectrum of rural poverty — providing not just resources, but the skills and knowledge to build lasting solutions from within communities.

Economic Justice
2
Strengthen Participation of Marginalized Rural Women

Ensure that the women most often left out of development programs — rural, low-income, with limited education — have access to training, resources, and platforms to participate and lead.

Inclusion
3
Advance Environmental Conservation & Sustainable Development

Promote sustainable land use, tree planting, grafted mango farming, and climate-resilient agricultural practices that protect the environment while generating food security and income.

Sustainability
4
Address Gender Inequality & the Feminization of Poverty

Directly confront the structural forces that keep women poor — through advocacy, paralegal training, community sensitization, and economic programs that build genuine financial independence.

Gender Equity
What We Believe

Our guiding values

🤝
Community Partnership
Communities are not recipients of development — they are its architects. BARWODA works with, not for, the communities it serves.
👑
Dignity First
Every person deserves to be treated with respect and dignity. Our programs are designed to build confidence and self-worth, not dependency.
🔬
Evidence & Learning
We track our impact rigorously and learn from every experience — continuously improving our programs based on what actually works in our communities.
🌐
Inclusion
Our work reaches the most marginalized — women and children in the deepest poverty. We are committed to leaving no one behind.
🔗
Collaboration
Real change requires partnership. We work with local government, international NGOs, and community leaders to maximize impact and scale our reach.
🌱
Long-Term Thinking
We invest in foundations — literacy, skills, rights knowledge — that empower communities to sustain development long after any project ends.
