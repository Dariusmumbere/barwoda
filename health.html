Program Overview

Healthcare as a
human right

In rural Kasese District, access to quality healthcare remains a significant challenge. BARWODA's Health Program takes a two-pronged approach: community health education that prevents illness before it starts, and the MaliMali Medical Centre in Kyarumba that provides direct clinical care for those in need.

The program also provides psychosocial support for vulnerable children, recognising that health encompasses mental and emotional well-being alongside physical care.

"Healthy women build healthy families. Healthy families build strong communities. Health is not a privilege — it is a foundation."
— BARWODA Health Philosophy

MaliMali Medical Centre

Clinical services at the
heart of the community

Located in Kyarumba, the MaliMali Medical Centre is BARWODA's flagship health facility — providing holistic, accessible care with a commitment to treating every patient with dignity and compassion.

🩺
Outpatient Services
General outpatient consultations for common illnesses, injuries, and preventable diseases. Open to community members of all ages, with particular attention to mothers and young children.
🏨
Inpatient Services
Inpatient ward providing continuous care for patients requiring observation, treatment, and recovery in a safe, supported environment close to their home communities.
🦷
Dental Care
Accessible dental services addressing pain relief, extractions, and basic oral health education — services rarely available in rural western Uganda without travelling long distances.
👁️
Eye Care
Vision screening, treatment for common eye conditions, and referrals for specialist care — helping community members maintain the sight they need for work, learning, and daily life.
Community Health Education

Preventing illness
before it starts

Beyond the clinic walls, BARWODA conducts regular community health education sessions that equip households with the knowledge to prevent common, preventable diseases.

  • Hygiene and sanitation practices
  • Safe water handling and household water treatment
  • Maternal and child nutrition guidance
  • Malaria prevention and vector control
  • HIV/AIDS awareness and prevention
  • Psychosocial support for vulnerable children
  • Collaboration with local government health structures
🧠
Psychosocial Support for Children
BARWODA recognises that health is not only physical. Children in difficult circumstances receive psychosocial support addressing trauma, grief, and the mental health challenges of poverty and instability.
🤝
Local Government Collaboration
BARWODA works alongside Kasese District health structures to ensure referrals, supply chains, and community health worker networks are strengthened rather than duplicated.
🌿
Holistic Care Model
MaliMali embraces a whole-person approach — addressing the physical, mental, and spiritual dimensions of health to deliver care that truly restores well-being.
